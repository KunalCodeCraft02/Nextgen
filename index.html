The North Star

Our Vision

Three pillars guiding every school toward a culture of innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurial excellence.

🌟

Build Young Innovators

Empowering school students to think creatively, identify real-life problems, and forge innovative solutions from the earliest stages of their journey.

🚀

Create a School Entrepreneurship Ecosystem

Establishing a working entrepreneurship cell that promotes innovation, collaboration, leadership, and entrepreneurial culture within every school campus.

🎯

Bridge Learning with Real-World Exposure

Providing real-world exposure at a young age to enhance soft skills — leadership, public speaking, event management, critical thinking, and entrepreneurial knowledge.

The Compass

Our Mission

NextGen Innovators is a school-level entrepreneurship initiative by E-Cell GCOE Kolhapur, designed to plant the seeds of entrepreneurial thinking in young minds.

We believe that the greatest ideas are born when students learn to see problems as opportunities — and are given the tools, mentorship, and platform to act on them.

I

Identify & Innovate

Train students to spot real-world problems and ideate breakthrough solutions using design thinking principles.

II

Collaborate & Lead

Build cross-grade squads that foster teamwork, communication, and leadership from day one.

III

Compete & Excel

Give students a stage to present, compete, and earn recognition at the prestigious E-Summit GCOE Kolhapur.

The grand launch — students form cross-grade innovation squads, ignite their entrepreneurial mindset through interactive sessions, and receive a real-world problem statement to tackle throughout the year.

Phase 2 · Event 1
🏫 Startup 101 Workshop
🗓 15th – 31st August
A full-day deep dive into entrepreneurship. Students learn to identify market gaps, brainstorm viable solutions, build resilience, and complete a one-page business model framework.
Problem SolvingBusiness ModellingResilience

Phase 2 · Event 3
💡 Idea Ignite: Junior

The Four Seas

Program Phases

A year-long odyssey structured across four purposeful phases, each building on the last.

I
Phase 1 · July
The Genesis
  • Genesis Bootcamp — Mindset activation, team formation, problem brief
  • Social Media Challenge — Build the school E-Cell's digital identity
🗓 1st – 31st July
II
Phase 2 · Aug–Sep
The Deep Dive
  • Startup 101 Workshop — Entrepreneurship fundamentals & business modelling
  • Idea Ignite: Junior — School-level pitch competition; top 5 qualify for E-Summit
🗓 15th Aug – Mid Sep
III
Phase 3 · Oct–Dec
The Horizon
  • Field Visit at E-Cell GCOEK — Free professional exposure during Diwali vacation

🗓 Diwali Vacation – Late December

Phase 4 · Grand Finale
The Grand Finale
